Cookie Policy – puriKRON

Last updated: 18 Jun 2026 • Official language: English

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help remember preferences and improve your experience.

2. How we use cookies

puriKRON uses minimal, functional storage only:

  • Language preference — stored in localStorage to remember your selected language (e.g. Turkish, English).
  • Consent record — stored in localStorage when you accept cookies and legal policies on the homepage.
  • Essential session data — no advertising or third-party tracking cookies are deployed by this site.

3. Third-party services

When you click external links (e.g. BaseScan, DEX aggregators), those services may set their own cookies. We do not control third-party cookie policies.

4. Managing cookies

You can clear site data via your browser settings. Clearing storage will reset your language preference.
