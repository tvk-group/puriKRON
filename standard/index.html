Core Rules

Seven non-negotiable launch rules

1
No presaleNo token sale before public liquidity. No early-access rounds, no whitelist discounts, no seed allocations sold to the public.
2
No private saleNo off-market deals, no insider allocations at preferential terms. All non-LP allocations are timelocked and publicly documented.
3
Fixed supply, no minting10 billion tokens per KRON Family memecoin. Supply is capped at deployment. No inflation, no hidden mint functions.
4
Standard tokenomics template80% fair-launch liquidity · 10% ecosystem (12-month timelock) · 5% founder (6-month lock) · 5% founder (12-month lock).
5
On-chain timelocksAll non-LP allocations enforced by immutable on-chain lock contracts. Addresses published on the official verification page.
6
Anti-dump transparencyAny sale from unlocked allocations requires 14 days public notice. Maximum 10% of available allocation per sale event.
7
Official verification before trustContract addresses published only through official domains after verification. If it is not on the verify page, it is not official.
